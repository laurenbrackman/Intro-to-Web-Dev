Hello there!

I'm Lauren

I'm a soon-to-be high school graduate (June 1st, 2019 but who's counting?) who loves sewing, adventures, and all things yellow. I started Happy Scrunchie in September of 2019, but my dream for the company started nearly a year earlier! It's been an incredible journey developing this business from the ground up and my excitement only grows with each new pattern released and sale made. I soon will be headed to college in Bozeman, MT, but rest assured, I have already mapped out a sewing corner in my dorm room for many more scrunchies to come. Thank you for checking out my website!
